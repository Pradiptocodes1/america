ROSE DAY

Dear Shaswata,

You are my rose, delicate yet so strong, beautiful in ways that unfold with every moment we share. I can't walk past a flower vendor anymore without my heart pulling me toward the roses, wanting to place one in your hands, even though I know no flower could ever come close to you.

The shabby little tissue rose we made that sat in my drawer, crumpled and imperfect, and every time I saw it, I can't help but smile. And speaking of things that need a little care, I have seen your poor charging cable with all those wires peeking out like wild little stems. You needed something to keep your world connected, to keep the energy flowing. Then there was that bloom I gave you that will never fade, the one that catches the light with a warm, lasting glow, just like what I feel for you. Oh, and that sweet moment we shared over a golden-topped treat, your smile worth more than anything.

Happy Rose Day, my forever bloom.

Yours forever
